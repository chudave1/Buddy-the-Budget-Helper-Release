# Privacy Policy — Buddy the Budget Helper

**Last updated:** June 18, 2026  
**Publisher:** David A Chu Consulting

## Summary

Buddy the Budget Helper is a packaged Windows application. **We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on our servers.** All budget and app data you create stays **on your PC** in the app's local storage.

## Information we do not collect

We do **not**:

- Operate online accounts or logins for this app
- Send your data to our servers or the cloud
- Sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing
- Use analytics or tracking SDKs to profile you

## Data on your device

The app stores information **only on your computer**, such as:

- Budget categories, amounts, and related records you enter
- Data imported from **files you choose** (for example CSV files on your PC)
- Local database and backup files created by the app in its install/data area

You control this data. You can back it up, restore it, or remove it by using the app's features or uninstalling the app.

**You** are responsible for any information you choose to type or import (for example account labels or transaction descriptions). We do not access that content remotely.

## Optional internet use

The app works **offline** for core features. The following optional features use the internet:

### Help

If you open **Help**, the app may open your default web browser to our public help/documentation page:

<https://chudave1.github.io/Buddy-the-Budget-Helper-Release/>

That visit is handled by your browser and its provider, not by us collecting data through the app.

### SimpleFIN integration (optional)

If you choose to set up [SimpleFIN integration](simplefin-integration.md), Buddy connects to the **SimpleFIN Bridge** over the internet when you import transactions or test an account connection. Buddy does **not** connect directly to your banks or other financial institutions; the SimpleFIN Bridge retrieves account data on your behalf according to the access you grant in your SimpleFIN subscription.

Transaction data returned by SimpleFIN is imported into Buddy and stored **locally on your PC**, the same as data from CSV files. We do not receive that data on our servers. Your SimpleFIN setup token, access URL, and related credentials are stored locally on your device as described in the SimpleFIN help topic. SimpleFIN is a separate third-party service with its own terms and privacy practices.

## Third parties

- **Microsoft Store:** Microsoft may process information related to purchase, download, updates, and Store policies. See the [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement).
- **SimpleFIN (optional):** If you enable SimpleFIN integration, Buddy sends requests to the SimpleFIN Bridge to retrieve transaction data you ask for. Buddy does not connect directly to your financial institutions; the Bridge handles that under your SimpleFIN subscription. We do not send your budget database to SimpleFIN, and we do not receive your SimpleFIN data on our servers. SimpleFIN operates independently and may process account and transaction data under its own terms. See [SimpleFIN integration](simplefin-integration.md) for how Buddy uses this feature and the [SimpleFIN privacy policy](https://beta-bridge.simplefin.org/info/privacy) for how SimpleFIN handles your data.
- **Chart/UI components:** The app uses licensed third-party UI libraries on your device only; we do not send your budget data to those vendors through the app.

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
